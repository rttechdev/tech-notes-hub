# Introduction to LangGraph

## What is LangGraph?

**LangGraph** is a framework for building **AI agentic workflows** in a structured, graph-based way.

It is widely used in production — companies use LangGraph at scale for real-world AI applications.

## Why LangGraph?

LangGraph solves key problems that arise when building complex AI agents:

- Makes agentic workflows **cleaner** and better organized
- Makes them more **maintainable** over time
- Makes them **easier to debug**
- Makes them more suitable for **production deployments**
